# Pricing Strategy Skill

## Pricing Models

### Fixed Price
Use for: well-defined scope, clear deliverables, migration phases, WAFR
Pros: predictable for customer, good margin if scoped well
Risk: scope creep — define exclusions explicitly

### Time & Materials
Use for: discovery phases, ambiguous scope, complex environments
Rate card (approximate):
- Senior Consultant / Architect: £900–1,200/day
- Consultant: £650–850/day
- Junior / Offshore (HabileLabs): £250–400/day

### Retainer (MSP)
Use for: ongoing managed services, FinOps, security monitoring
Structure: monthly fixed fee, define scope, include SLA
Minimum retainer: £2,000/month
Target MSP customer: £3,000–8,000/month

### Value-Based
Use for: Agent Bakery, large transformations
Price to the value delivered (cost savings × 12 months × 0.3)
Example: customer saving £5K/month → £18K engagement is well-positioned

## AWS Funding Offset
Always show the net customer cost after funding:
- Gross SOW value: £50,000
- MAP credits applied: -£15,000
- Net customer cost: £35,000
This dramatically improves conversion.

## Discount Rules (AMBER — Steve approval required)
- Up to 10%: Oliver can approve for strategic reasons
- 10–20%: Steve approval required
- Over 20%: Not without board discussion

## Never Do
- Discount before the customer asks
- Give a range ("£30–50K") — always anchor high with a clear scope
- Price in USD for UK customers
