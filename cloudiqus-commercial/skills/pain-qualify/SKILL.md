# PAIN Qualification Skill

## 100-Point Lead Score

| Dimension | Max | What earns points |
|-----------|-----|-------------------|
| Company Fit | 25 | SMB/education, UK/EMEA, AWS user, right headcount |
| Pain Signal | 25 | VMware pain, high bill, security incident, AI pressure |
| Funding Potential | 20 | MAP eligible, WAFR possible, POC fundable |
| Authority | 20 | C-suite engaged, budget confirmed, decision timeline |
| Timing | 10 | Active project, budget cycle, renewal imminent |

**Thresholds:**
- 75–100: 🔴 HOT — register ACE today
- 50–74: 🟡 WARM — qualify further in 48 hours
- 25–49: 🟢 NURTURE — 60-day sequence
- 0–24: ⚫ DISQUALIFY — log and move on

## PAIN Interview (in order)
**P — Problem:** What's the biggest issue? What have you tried? How long has this been a problem?
**A — Authority:** Who else is involved? Has budget been allocated?
**I — Impact:** What does this cost monthly? What does solving it unlock?
**N — Need/Timeline:** Is there a trigger driving this? What's your ideal timeline?

## Output: Qualification Summary
1. Score (0–100) with dimension breakdown
2. Verdict: HOT / WARM / NURTURE / DISQUALIFY
3. Top 3 confirmed pain signals
4. Funding programmes identified + immediate actions
5. Gaps to fill before SOW
6. Single best next action
