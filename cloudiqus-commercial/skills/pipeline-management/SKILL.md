# Pipeline Management Skill

## Pipeline Stages
| Stage | Definition | SLA to next stage |
|-------|-----------|------------------|
| Prospect | Identified, not yet contacted | — |
| Outreach | In 5-touch sequence | 21 days |
| Engaged | Replied or connected | 7 days |
| Discovery | Call booked or held | 5 days to qualify |
| Qualified | PAIN score 50+ | 14 days to SOW |
| SOW Sent | SOW delivered to customer | 14 days to follow up |
| Negotiation | Commercial discussion active | 7 days |
| Closed Won | Signed SOW | Kick off within 10 days |
| Closed Lost | Log reason, add to nurture | — |
| Nurture | PAIN score 25–49, 60-day cadence | — |

## Weekly Pipeline Review (Oliver runs this, Steve reviews)
Every Monday:
1. Deals at SOW Sent — follow up or close
2. Deals at Qualified — SOW ready or blocked?
3. Deals at Discovery — qualified or disqualify?
4. Outreach metrics vs targets
5. New deals to add from the week

## CRM Hygiene Rules
- Every deal must have: company, contact, stage, value, next action, next action date
- No deal stays in Qualified for more than 14 days without a SOW or disqualify decision
- ACE registered for every HOT deal — same day
- Funding eligibility noted on every deal record

## Revenue Forecasting
- Closed Won = 100% of value
- Negotiation = 80% probability
- SOW Sent = 50% probability
- Qualified = 25% probability
- Earlier stages = 0%
