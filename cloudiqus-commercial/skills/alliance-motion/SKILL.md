# Alliance Motion Skill

## The AWS Alliance Hierarchy (who does what)
| Role | Who | What they can do for CloudiQS |
|------|-----|-------------------------------|
| PDM (Partner Development Manager) | Our main AWS contact | POC approval, MAP fast-track, MDF, co-sell intro |
| PAM (Partner Account Manager) | Strategic relationship | Tier upgrades, competency guidance, PGP |
| PSA (Partner Solutions Architect) | Technical | Joint customer calls, architecture validation |
| AWS Sales Rep (AE) | Customer-side | Co-sell on large deals, executive briefings |

## When to Engage PDM (do this, not just when you need something)
- Monthly: pipeline update — share top 3 HOT deals, migration opportunities, any Agentic AI POCs
- Immediately: any deal >$50K, any MAP-eligible migration, any GenAI POC needing credits
- Proactively: send customer success stories — PDM needs them to justify CloudiQS investment
- Never: surprise PDM with a claim or credit request they haven't heard about

## Co-Sell Triggers (when to flag for AWS co-sell)
| Situation | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| Deal > $100K | Flag to PDM — invite AWS AE onto next call |
| Customer wants AWS executive presence | Request through PDM — 2 weeks lead time |
| Competitive situation (Azure/GCP) | AWS will co-sell hard — flag immediately |
| Migration > $500K AWS spend | PDM can unlock MAP fast-track and senior support |
| Agentic AI POC in regulated sector | AWS loves this — PDM can accelerate and co-market |

## ACE Co-Sell vs Partner-Led
- **Partner-led:** CloudiQS runs the deal. AWS supports on request. Best for <$50K.
- **Co-sell:** AWS AE engaged, joint pursuit. Best for >$100K or strategic accounts.
- **AWS-referred:** AWS brings CloudiQS a customer. Requires strong PDM relationship and visible pipeline.
- Rule: register ALL deals as ACE, even partner-led. This is what builds the co-sell relationship over time.

## The Alliance Play for an Agentic AI Opportunity
1. Qualify the opportunity (PAIN score 50+)
2. Register ACE — type based on scope
3. Brief PDM: "We have a GenAI POC with [customer] — requesting POC credit approval"
4. Get written approval from PDM before SOW goes out
5. Flag for co-sell if customer is >$50K or regulated sector
6. After delivery: submit case study to PDM for competency validation
7. Claim MDF for any co-marketing activity around the delivery
