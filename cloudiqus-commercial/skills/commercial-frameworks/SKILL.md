# Commercial Frameworks Skill

## PAIN (CloudiQS primary framework)
Problem → Authority → Impact → Need/Timeline
Best for: all CloudiQS conversations, especially first discovery calls
Why: simple, forces qualification before pitching

## MEDDIC (for complex, larger deals)
Metrics · Economic Buyer · Decision Criteria · Decision Process · Identify Pain · Champion
Best for: deals >£50K, enterprise customers, multi-stakeholder decisions
Use when: PAIN qualification reveals complex buying process

## Challenger Sale
Teach → Tailor → Take Control
Best for: outreach to prospects who don't know they have a problem
How we use it: "Most AWS environments have 3+ critical misconfigurations" (Teach) → tailor to their sector → propose the assessment

## Value Selling
Quantify the problem → quantify the solution → prove ROI
Best for: CFO conversations, board-level decisions
How we use it: "Your AWS bill is £X/month. We typically find 25% waste. That's £Y/month saved. Our engagement costs £Z. Payback in W months."

## When to Use Which
| Situation | Framework |
|-----------|----------|
| Cold outreach | Challenger (teach first) |
| First discovery call | PAIN |
| Large complex deal | MEDDIC |
| CFO/finance conversation | Value Selling |
| Objection handling | PAIN (restate their impact) |
