# SDR Outreach Skill

## The 4 Hooks (ranked by current strength)
1. **VMware/Broadcom** ← STRONGEST — licence costs 2–5x at renewal
2. **AWS Cost Savings** — 20–40% overspend is typical, we prove it in 30 days
3. **Cloud Security** — most AWS environments have at least one critical misconfiguration
4. **Agentic AI** — production agents, not chatbots, built on AWS Bedrock

## Hook Selection Guide
| Signal | Use Hook |
|--------|---------|
| VMware in job listings or tech stack | VMware/Broadcom |
| No MSP, cloud-heavy, scaling fast | Cost Savings |
| Financial services, healthcare, data-sensitive | Security |
| Digital-first, AI ambitions in messaging | Agent Bakery |

## Persona Table
| Title | Pain | Hook | Tone |
|-------|------|------|------|
| CTO/VP Eng | Tech debt, team capacity | VMware or AI | Peer, technical |
| CFO/FD | Unpredictable cloud bills | Cost Savings | ROI, concise |
| CEO/MD (SMB) | Growth bottlenecks | Cost or AI | Simple, outcomes |
| IT Director | Vendor lock-in, operational burden | VMware or Security | Empathetic |
| Head of Digital | Competitive pressure, AI readiness | Agent Bakery | Visionary |

## Email Structure
```
Subject: [Specific to their situation]
[Opening — one sentence, their specific pain]
[Body — 2–3 sentences. One proof point. One AWS angle.]
[CTA — single, low friction]
[Signature — name, CloudiQS, AWS Advanced Partner]
```

## Hard Rules
- Max 100 words in body
- Subject must reference something company/sector-specific
- One CTA only — never give a choice
- Never attach anything to first email
- Never use: synergies, leverage, solutions, touch base, circle back
- Always include AWS Advanced Partner credential
