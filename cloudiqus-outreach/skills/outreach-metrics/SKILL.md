# Outreach Metrics Skill

## Weekly Oliver Scorecard

| Metric | Target | Action if below |
|--------|--------|----------------|
| Emails sent | 25/week | Review target list, add more |
| LinkedIn connections sent | 20/week | Review persona targeting |
| Reply rate (email) | >8% | Review hook and subject line |
| LinkedIn acceptance rate | >30% | Review connection note |
| Discovery calls booked | 2/week | Review CTA and follow-up timing |
| Leads scored 50+ | 4/month | Review qualification criteria |
| SOWs sent | 1/month | Review pipeline conversion |

## Benchmarks (AWS consulting, UK SMB)
- Cold email open rate: 35–45% is good
- Reply rate: 8–12% is good
- Call booking from email: 2–4% is good
- LinkedIn acceptance: 25–35% is good

## Decision Triggers
- Reply rate <5% for 2 weeks → rewrite the hook email
- No discovery calls for 2 weeks → review CTA and follow-up sequence
- Pipeline stalled at qualification → review PAIN question depth
- Conversion from call to SOW <25% → review discovery quality

## Output Format
Weekly: produce a one-page outreach scorecard with actuals vs targets and one recommended action.
