# LinkedIn Sequence Skill

## The 5-Touch Sequence

| Touch | Day | Type | Content |
|-------|-----|------|---------|
| 1 | D0 | Connection request | 30-word personalised note — no pitch |
| 2 | D3 | Message after connect | One sector insight — no ask |
| 3 | D7 | Email (parallel) | Hook email — one problem, one proof |
| 4 | D14 | LinkedIn message | Case study or funding angle |
| 5 | D21 | LinkedIn message | Soft close or value share |

## Touch 1 — Connection Note (30 words max)
Template: "Hi [name] — I work with [sector] businesses on AWS infrastructure. Saw [specific thing about them]. Would love to connect."
- Never mention pitch, call, or meeting
- Always reference something specific

## Touch 2 — Warm Message (after they accept)
Template: "Thanks for connecting. Interesting timing — we're seeing a lot of [sector] companies dealing with [relevant pain]. Happy to share some notes from recent work if useful."
- One sentence. No ask. Opens a door.

## Touch 4 — Case Study or Funding Angle
"We recently helped a [similar company] [specific outcome — save £X/month / migrate 50 VMs in 6 weeks / get £25K AWS WAFR credits]. Happy to share the approach if relevant to you."

## Touch 5 — Soft Close
"Happy to keep sharing insights — but if there's ever a moment where AWS costs, security, or AI are on the agenda, I'd love 20 minutes. If not, no worries at all."

## Hard Rules
- Never pitch on Touch 1 or Touch 2
- Never send more than one message per week
- Stop the sequence if they reply — move to discovery-prep
- Log every touch in CRM/pipeline-management
