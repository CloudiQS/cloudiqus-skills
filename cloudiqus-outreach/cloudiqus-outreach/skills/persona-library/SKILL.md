# Persona Library

## Persona 1: CTO / VP Engineering
**Pain:** Technical debt, team capacity, vendor lock-in, keeping up with AWS releases
**Buys when:** Solution saves engineering time, reduces operational risk, has credibility
**Language:** Architecture, scalability, reliability, DevOps, Kubernetes, IaC
**Channel:** LinkedIn, technical blogs, AWS re:Invent
**What wins them:** Ex-AWS credibility, technical depth in the conversation, reference architecture
**Hook:** VMware or Agent Bakery
**Ask:** 30-min architecture conversation, not a sales call

## Persona 2: CFO / Finance Director
**Pain:** Unpredictable cloud bills, no visibility, cost allocation across teams
**Buys when:** Clear ROI, fast payback, low risk, board-ready numbers
**Language:** Cost per unit, budget, CapEx vs OpEx, waste, efficiency
**Channel:** Email, LinkedIn, CFO networks
**What wins them:** Specific £ savings, fast time-to-value, funded engagement angle
**Hook:** Cost Savings
**Ask:** "We'll show you where the waste is — free 30-min review"

## Persona 3: IT Director
**Pain:** Vendor lock-in, support burden, security risk, board pressure
**Buys when:** Reduces their personal risk, has good support model, proven delivery
**Language:** SLA, uptime, patching, disaster recovery, vendor management
**Channel:** Email, LinkedIn, IT peer networks
**What wins them:** MSP credibility, case studies, clear handover/support model
**Hook:** VMware or Security
**Ask:** Risk-free assessment

## Persona 4: CEO / MD (SMB)
**Pain:** Growth bottlenecks, cost control, competitive pressure, AI FOMO
**Buys when:** Simple outcome, fast, trusted, not too technical
**Language:** Customers, revenue, competitive edge, risk, cost
**Channel:** LinkedIn, referrals, events
**What wins them:** Short story, peer reference, outcome-first
**Hook:** Cost Savings or Agent Bakery
**Ask:** 20-minute intro, CEO to CEO

## Persona 5: Head of Digital / Chief Digital Officer
**Pain:** Competitive pressure, AI readiness, slow internal delivery
**Buys when:** Innovative, credible, fast to deploy, strategic fit
**Language:** Digital transformation, AI, automation, competitive advantage
**Channel:** LinkedIn, AI conferences, industry events
**What wins them:** Agent Bakery demo, vision alignment, quick POC
**Hook:** Agent Bakery
**Ask:** "Let us show you a working agent in your environment in 2 weeks"
