# Account Intelligence Skill

## Account Health Dimensions
| Dimension | Green | Amber | Red |
|-----------|-------|-------|-----|
| AWS spend trend | Increasing | Flat | Decreasing |
| Services used | Expanding | Stable | Contracting |
| Stakeholder engagement | Monthly touchpoint | Quarterly | No contact |
| Support tickets | Low, resolved fast | Medium | High, slow resolve |
| NPS / satisfaction | Promoter | Passive | Detractor |
| Contract renewal | >6 months away | 3–6 months | <3 months |

## Expansion Signal Types
| Signal | Potential Expansion | Value |
|--------|-------------------|-------|
| Rightsizing >£500/month | Optimisation engagement | £10–30K |
| New business unit on AWS | MSP expansion | +£2–5K/month MRR |
| Security finding | Security assessment + remediation | £10–40K |
| AI ambition mentioned | Agent Bakery POC | £15–50K |
| New acquisition | Migration engagement | £30–150K |
| VMware still in environment | Migration | £50–300K |

## Stakeholder Map
For each account, identify:
- **Economic Buyer:** holds the budget
- **Technical Champion:** advocates for CloudiQS internally
- **End Users:** benefit from the work
- **Blocker:** who might oppose renewal or expansion

## Quarterly Account Review Agenda (internal)
1. Health score review
2. Expansion signals identified
3. Renewal risk assessment
4. Stakeholder engagement plan
5. Next commercial conversation owner and date
