# Company Research Skill

## Research Depth Levels

### Level 1 — Outreach (5 minutes)
- Sector, size, location
- Top buying signal
- Target persona
- One conversation starter

### Level 2 — Discovery Prep (15 minutes)
- Full tech stack analysis
- Decision maker identified with LinkedIn
- Recent news and triggers
- AWS footprint indicators
- Current cloud spend estimate

### Level 3 — Account Intelligence (30 minutes)
- Full org structure relevant to the deal
- Budget cycle and procurement process
- Incumbent vendors and renewal dates
- Competitive situation
- Multi-year expansion potential

## Tech Stack Detection Sources
- LinkedIn job listings: infrastructure, DevOps, cloud roles
- BuiltWith.com: web technology stack
- Stackshare.io: engineering stack
- Companies House: financials and director history (UK)
- Crunchbase: funding, team size, technology tags
- G2: software stack from reviews

## Output
Level-appropriate research pack with:
1. Company snapshot
2. Tech stack confidence: HIGH / MEDIUM / LOW + evidence
3. Top buying signal + source
4. Best persona to target (name if findable)
5. Conversation opener
