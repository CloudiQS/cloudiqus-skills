# Buying Signals Skill

## Signal Types and Response Times

### 🔴 Act within 24 hours
| Signal | Source | Action |
|--------|--------|--------|
| Hiring DevOps/Cloud Architect | LinkedIn Jobs | Research + outreach |
| VMware renewal coming up | LinkedIn post, news | VMware angle email |
| Security breach reported | News, Glassdoor | Security angle |
| New CTO/CDO appointed | LinkedIn | Introduction outreach |
| Series A/B funding round | Crunchbase | Activate + growth angle |

### 🟡 Act within 1 week
| Signal | Source | Action |
|--------|--------|--------|
| AWS re:Invent mentions in their content | Twitter/X, blog | AI/GenAI angle |
| Job listing for "AI strategy" or "ML engineer" | LinkedIn Jobs | Agent Bakery angle |
| Office expansion or new location | LinkedIn, news | Infrastructure growth angle |
| Acquisition or merger | News | Migration/consolidation angle |

### 🟢 Monitor and nurture
| Signal | Source | Action |
|--------|--------|--------|
| Company posts about cloud journey | LinkedIn | Engage with comment |
| Employee reviews mention AWS costs | Glassdoor | Cost savings angle |
| Following AWS LinkedIn page | LinkedIn | Add to warm sequence |

## Signal Monitoring Setup
- Set Google Alerts for: [target company] + AWS/cloud/VMware/digital
- LinkedIn Sales Navigator: trigger on job listings and company news
- Crunchbase: funding alerts for target companies
- Glassdoor: manual review for IT/engineering culture signals
