## PDM (Partner Development Manager) Relationship
- PDM = our main AWS contact for co-sell and funding
- Keep PDM updated on all HOT deals and migration opportunities
- Invite PDM to customer calls when deal >$50K or customer wants AWS exec presence
- PDM can unlock: POC credits, MAP fast-track, executive briefings, AWS funding

## ACE Co-Sell Motions
- Partner-led: CloudiQS runs the deal, AWS provides support
- Co-sell: AWS sales rep engaged, joint pursuit
- AWS-referred: AWS brings us a customer lead
- Best for CloudiQS: start partner-led, invite co-sell for deals >$100K
