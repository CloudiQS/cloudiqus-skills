# AWS Activate Skill

## What It Is
AWS Activate gives qualifying startups up to $100,000 in AWS credits, plus technical support and training.
CloudiQS registers as an Activate Provider — our startup customers apply through our link.

## Eligibility (customer must meet)
- VC-backed, accelerator-backed, or early-stage startup
- Not yet a significant AWS customer (limited prior spend)
- Incorporated entity
- Using or planning to use AWS

## Credit Tiers
| Tier | Credits | Who |
|------|---------|-----|
| Activate Founders | $1,000 | Self-funded, very early |
| Activate | Up to $100,000 | VC-backed, accelerator |

## CloudiQS Process
1. Register CloudiQS as Activate Provider (one-time, via AWS Partner Central)
2. When qualifying customer identified → provide them our Provider link
3. Customer applies → CloudiQS gets credit for the referral
4. Credits applied to customer's AWS account — reduces their AWS bill

## When to Use
- New SaaS startup customer
- Customer is raising or recently raised VC funding
- Customer has limited current AWS spend
- Engagement includes building their AWS foundation

## Talking Point
"If you're VC-backed, you may qualify for up to $100K in AWS credits through our Activate Provider status. That could fund your first 12 months of AWS costs — let me check your eligibility."
