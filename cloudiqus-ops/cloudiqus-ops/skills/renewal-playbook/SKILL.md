# Renewal Playbook Skill

## Renewal Timeline

| Days to Renewal | Action | Owner |
|----------------|--------|-------|
| R-180 | Flag renewal on radar, check health score | Oliver |
| R-90 | QBR scheduled, value summary prepared | Oliver + Delivery |
| R-60 | Renewal conversation started, expansion discussed | Oliver |
| R-30 | Renewal terms agreed or escalated | Steve |
| R-14 | Contract sent for signature | Oliver |
| R-0 | Contract signed, new period starts | Oliver |
| R+7 if not signed | Steve personal call to customer | Steve |

## Expansion at Renewal
Every renewal is an expansion conversation. Prepare:
- Total value delivered in £ (cost savings + security findings + delivery)
- 1–2 expansion options (new service, more accounts, Agent Bakery)
- AWS funding available for expansion (MAP if new workloads, WAFR credits)

## Renewal Risk Signals
- Health score drops to RED within 90 days of renewal
- Stakeholder change (new CTO, new CFO)
- Budget freeze mentioned
- Competitor engagement detected

## Escalation
If renewal not progressing by R-30 → Steve takes over personally.
If customer at risk of churn → full retention plan with Steve, delivery lead, Oliver.
