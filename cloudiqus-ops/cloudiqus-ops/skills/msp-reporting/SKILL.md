# MSP Reporting Skill

## Customer-Facing Reports

### Monthly Value Report (per customer)
Cadence: by 5th of each month
Format: 1–2 page PDF or structured email
Sections: cost summary · anomalies · rightsizing · budget status · actions taken · recommended next steps
Recipient: customer technical contact + sponsor
Produced by: FinOps agent (automated) or delivery lead

### Quarterly Business Review (QBR)
Cadence: quarterly, scheduled in advance
Format: 30-min call + 5-page deck
Sections: health score review · value delivered (£) · roadmap · expansion opportunities · renewal discussion
Recipient: customer sponsor + economic buyer
Led by: Steve or Oliver

## Internal Reports

### Weekly Pipeline Report (Monday)
Oliver produces: outreach metrics · pipeline stage summary · deals needing action · funding status
Recipients: Steve

### Monthly Business Report (1st of month)
Sections: MRR snapshot · new customers · churned (if any) · AWS funding received · pipeline value · top 3 actions
Recipients: Steve (+ Andrea Zhang if financial review month)

### AWS Partner Scorecard (quarterly)
Sections: tier requirements · competency health · cert headcount · ACE pipeline · funding received
Recipients: Steve + PDM
