# Customer Success Skill

## The 4 Post-Launch Tracks

### Track 1 — Value Realisation (Month 1–3)
First win documented · First Value Report delivered · Quick wins implemented · Sponsor briefed on ROI

### Track 2 — Expansion (Month 3–9)
Health score green · Expansion signals acted on · Expansion SOW sent · Second service added

### Track 3 — Retention (Month 9–12)
QBR with value summary · Renewal conversation started at R-90 · Contract renewed · Expansion at renewal

### Track 4 — Advocacy (Month 12+)
NPS collected · Case study written · AWS reference submitted · Speaking opportunity explored

## NPS Collection
Cadence: 6 months post-onboarding, then annually
Method: short email survey (2 questions)
1. "On a scale of 0–10, how likely are you to recommend CloudiQS to a peer?"
2. "What's the main reason for your score?"
Score threshold: 8+ → request case study and reference · 6–7 → ask what would improve score · 0–5 → escalate to Steve immediately

## Reference Programme
Good reference = customer who will:
- Speak to a prospect on a call
- Be named in a case study
- Be submitted to AWS as a competency reference

Ask at the right moment: after a clear win, after NPS 9–10, never before Day 90.
